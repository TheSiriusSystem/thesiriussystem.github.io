Hobbies & Interests

Video games and programming are where my passion lies; I tend to game a lot and/or code whatever pops up in my mind! Games, software, game mods, etc... Whenever I'm not busy with either, you may sometimes find me indulging in either drawing or walking.

I have a fondness for art, especially those that give off a surreal feeling. While I can sorta draw pixel art, it isn't my strongest suit in general! I am more adept at editing images than making new stuff.

Lastly, music makes up a portion of my daily life! And I also happen to hold a special place for the outdoors in my heart, especially the greens. I prefer walking close to greenery and appreciating the view whenever possible.

Mission

My mission is simple. Alongside constantly refining my skills, I aspire to be a game developer first and foremost: jack-of-all-trades of the programming world second. Additionally, contributing to open-source projects is definitely something on my mind despite not being a priority.
